# 🧩 2. What is a System?

A system is:

```text id="s7y3pk"
Input → Processing → Output
```

---

### In AI music:

```text id="m9k2qv"
User Idea → AI Processing → Final Song
```

---

👉 But real systems are NOT this simple

---

---

# 🔍 3. Break the System into Layers

To design properly, divide into layers:

---

## 🧾 3.1 Input Layer

* User prompt
* Mood, genre, language
* Optional: lyrics

---

---

## 🧠 3.2 Intelligence Layer

* Text understanding (LLM)
* Music planning (latent structure)

---

---

## 🎵 3.3 Generation Layer

* Music model (Transformer / Diffusion / API)
* Voice generation (optional)

---

---

## 🎚️ 3.4 Processing Layer

* Mixing
* Enhancement
* Trimming

---

---

## 📤 3.5 Output Layer

* Audio file
* Download / playback
* Variations

---

---

### Full view:

```text id="u8f3zd"
Input
 ↓
Understanding
 ↓
Planning
 ↓
Generation
 ↓
Processing
 ↓
Output
```

# 🧠 4. Step-by-Step Design Method

Use this framework every time:

---

## ✅ Step 1: Define the Goal

---

### Example:

```text id="z5k1pr"
"Generate Bengali devotional songs based on user mood"
```

---

👉 Be specific

---

---

## ✅ Step 2: Define Inputs

---

```text id="t7m4qs"
- Mood (calm, emotional)
- Theme (Krishna, Durga)
- Language (Bengali)
```

---

---

## ✅ Step 3: Define Outputs

---

```text id="j9p2fd"
- Full song (audio)
- Lyrics
- Optional variations
```

---

---

## ✅ Step 4: Design Pipeline

---

```text id="r4x8mn"
Prompt
 ↓
LLM → generate lyrics
 ↓
Music model → generate instrumental
 ↓
Voice model → singing
 ↓
Audio processing → polish
```

---

---

## ✅ Step 5: Add Evaluation Layer

---

```text id="k3p7yc"
Generate multiple outputs
 ↓
Score (quality + creativity)
 ↓
Select best
```

---

---

## ✅ Step 6: Add Control Mechanisms

---

* Prompt templates
* Style constraints (raga, genre)
* Parameter tuning

---

---

# 🚧 7. Common Design Mistakes

---

## ❌ 1. Thinking only about model

> “I just need a good model”

---

👉 Reality:

> You need a **good system**

---

---

## ❌ 2. No evaluation step

* Leads to inconsistent output

---

---

## ❌ 3. Weak input design

* Bad prompts → bad results

---

---

## ❌ 4. Ignoring post-processing

* Output feels raw and unpolished

---

---
