## **Chapter 1.6 — Prompt Engineering Basics**

---

### 🎯 What You Will Learn in This Chapter

* How to control music using **prompts**
* The 3 most important elements:

  * **Mood**
  * **Instrument**
  * **Style**
* How to combine them for better results

---

## 🧠 1. What is Prompt Engineering?

### Simple Idea

Prompt engineering means:

👉 **Writing better instructions to get better results**

---

### 🧠 Real-Life Analogy

Think of talking to a musician:

* “Play music” ❌ (too vague)
* “Play a sad piano tune slowly” ✅ (clear instruction)

👉 AI behaves the same way

---

### 💡 One-Line Definition

> Prompt engineering = **the art of describing what you want clearly**

---

## 🎭 2. Mood (Emotion of Music)

### What is Mood?

Mood is:

👉 **How the music feels emotionally**

---

### 🎧 Common Mood Words

* Happy 😊
* Sad 😢
* Calm 🌿
* Energetic ⚡
* Romantic ❤️
* Dark 🌑
* Epic 🎬

---

### 🧠 Example

```
happy piano melody
```

👉 Sounds light, cheerful

```
sad piano melody
```

👉 Sounds slow, emotional

---

### 🎯 Key Insight

👉 Same instrument + different mood = completely different music

---

---

### 🎧 Common Instruments

* Piano
* Guitar
* Violin
* Flute
* Drums
* Tabla
* Synth

---

### 🧠 Example

```id="y5d9kq"

```

vs

```id="n3v7rs"

```

👉 Same mood, different feel

---

### 🎯 Key Insight

## 🎼 4. Style (Type or Genre of Music)

![Image](https://cdn.vectorstock.com/i/500p/35/17/music-genre-icon-set-diverse-styles-vector-60303517.jpg)

### What is Style?

Style means:

👉 **The category or genre of music**

---

### 🎧 Common Styles

* Classical
* Bollywood
* Jazz
* Rock
* Electronic (EDM)
* Ambient
* Folk

---

### 🧠 Example

```id="4a7kpm"
happy piano classical music
```

vs

```id="z1q8cv"
happy piano jazz music
```

👉 Same instrument, different style

---

### 🎯 Key Insight

👉 Style defines the **overall structure and rhythm**

---

## 🧩 5. Combining Mood + Instrument + Style

This is where real power comes.

---

### 💡 Simple Formula

```id="bq2m7x"
[Mood] + [Instrument] + [Style]
```

---

### 🎧 Examples

* “sad violin classical music”
* “happy guitar bollywood style song”
* “calm flute ambient music”
* “energetic synth electronic dance music”

---

### 🧠 Real-Life Analogy

Like ordering tea:

* “tea” ❌
* “hot ginger milk tea” ✅

👉 More detail = better output

---

## 🔁 6. Same Idea, Different Results

Let’s change only one part:

---

### 🎯 Example 1 — Change Mood

* “happy piano music”
* “sad piano music”

👉 Emotion changes

---

### 🎯 Example 2 — Change Instrument

* “happy piano music”
* “happy flute music”

👉 Sound texture changes

---

### 🎯 Example 3 — Change Style

* “happy piano classical music”
* “happy piano jazz music”

👉 Structure changes

---

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes

❌ Too vague:

```id="qk7vxn"
music
```

❌ Too confusing:

```id="r8mz2p"
happy sad fast slow music
```

---

## 💡 Pro Tips

### 1. Use Clear Words

👉 Avoid mixing opposite emotions

---

### 2. Start Small

👉 Then add details gradually

---

### 3. Think Like a Listener

👉 “What should this music feel like?”
